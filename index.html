Algo Jam

It is an algorithm based event divided into 3 rounds. First round will be an online round organised on HackerEarth platform on 8th January from 8am-12:30pm. Second and Third Round will be held at Maharaja Surajmal Institute of Technology, Janakpuri on 10th and 11th January respectively from 10:00am. Winners will be declared after the completion of third round.

Rules

  • - Refrain from discussing strategy during the contest.
  • - Any case of code plagiarism will result in disqualification.
  • - Decision of event heads will be final and irrevocable.
  • - Before you attempt programming questions, you should choose a language from the given list.
  • - All inputs for the programming problems are from STDIN and output to STDOUT.
  • - To understand more about the judging environment, time limits, etc. you can read information about the HackerEarth judge.
  • - Programming questions have a Compile & Run option which you can use to run your solution against sample test cases before submitting it.
  • - You can submit the solution for a programming problem as many times as you want. For subjective and objective problems, you can change your answer as many times as you want.

Android Gaming

As the name suggests, this event calls out for all the gamers who love gaming on their mobile phones. There are a total of 6 games, 3 multiplayer and 3 single player games. Multiplayer games include – Mini Militia, Glow Hockey, and Asphalt Nitro. Single player games include – mmm fingers, Sine line, Piano tiles. This event will be held at Maharaja Surajmal Institue of Technology, Janakpuri on 10th and 11th of January from 10:00AM.

Rules

  • - Decision of Event Heads will be final and irrevocable.
  • - Your version of game will be deleted and we will provide the game version from our side.

BrainStorm

It is an online quizzing event based on famous personalities, TV shows, movies, places and everything your mind can boggle upon. There will be a total of 25 questions and solving each question promotes you to the next level. The victor will be the one who solves all the 25 questions in the least time. This event will start on 7th January at 12 noon and will end on 10th January at 8:00pm

Rules

  • - Questions can be simple text, image, audio or video.
  • - Decision of event heads will be final and irrevocable.
