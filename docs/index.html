Line Following Bot

The autonomous robot will begin its run from an initial marked position in a dark isolated room. It will follow the line strip beneath it and would cover the track. The robot has to decide its path by sensing the line. The objective is to cover the entire track in least possible time with sufficient accuracy.

Rounds :

Round 1

  • - There will be a single track.
  • - There is no such limit on the time of completion of the bot run however the team which completes the run in minimum time qualifies for the next round.
  • - Maximum of 8 hand touches (but with time penalty) and one hand pick (with extra time penalty) is allowed throughout the round in order to adjust its position if it is heading wrong.
  • - There will be one checkpoint and if it gets skipped, additional penalty will be charged accordingly.
  • - The team cannot make any programming changes in the course once the bot starts its run.
  • - Calibration time will be provided before the competition.

Round 2

  • - Track complexity increases this time.
  • - Each team will have maximum of 5 minutes to complete the track.
  • - Maximum of 6 hand touches (but with penalty) and 2 hand picks (with extra penalty) are allowed throughout the round in order to adjust its position if it is heading wrong.
  • - There will be four checkpoints and maximum two can be skipped but penalty will be charged accordingly.
  • - The team cannot make any programming changes in the course once the bot starts its run.

Rules :

  • - Decision of chief organizers and event heads is final and every participant has to accept it.
  • - Round 1 is completing the track efficiently and in less time.
  • - Clearing round 1 in an average minimum stipulated time ensures qualification to the next round.
  • - Each team can have maximum of 4 members.
  • - Each team should have unique participants i.e. it cannot have any common member.
  • - Cross college teams are allowed.
  • - The Right spirit of participation and discipline (during the competition) is expected from the participants.

Event :

  • - There will be a line present in the arena. This line can be white on black background or black on white background.
  • - The thickness of line will not be the same.
  • - The track will have breaks in it i.e. gaps.
  • - In case the robot is not able to cross the gap they can reposition it but marks will be deducted for it.

Restart & Timeout :

  • - A maximum of 1 Timeouts of 5 Minutes each may be taken in the second round only.
  • - A penalty will be rewarded for each Timeout taken by the team and the robots will start from the start zone.
  • - The participants can have a maximum of 2 restarts for rounds. A penalty will be imposed for each restart.

Junkyard

Round 1 (Day 1) :

Time per participation: 5-6 minutes.
Qualified teams: 10
Games: Dr. Driving / Paper plane / flip the bottle (tentative)

Each team will get a chance to play the games mentioned above. In Dr. Driving the teams have to make the RC car cross a track full of hurdles in minimum time. The points will be awarded as per the time taken. In paper plane game, the teams have to try and fly the paper plane through the hole in the cardboard sheet. The points will be awarded as per which hole the plane went through. The third and the last game would be the flip the bottle game. The teams will be awarded as per the number of successful bottle flips. The cumulative score of the three games will be calculated and recorded. The top 10 teams will qualify for the second round.

Round 2 (Day 1) :

Time per participation: 10-12 minutes
Qualified teams: 5
Games: Dart game / Ping-pong basketball

This round consists of 1vs1 elimination wherein the 10 teams will play against each other such that only 5 teams will qualify to the final round.
The elimination will take place in a way that number 1 team in Round 1 will play with number 2 team in Round 1, number 3 with 4 and so on. Losing will lead to direct elimination. In the dart game the teams will have to hit the target board and try to score the maximum points. In the ping-pong basketball game, the teams will have to score points by putting the ping-pong ball in the basket. The cumulative score of the two games will decide the winner. The 5 winner teams will go to the next round.

Final Round (Day 1) :

25-30 minutes
Qualified teams: 1
Games: Best out of waste

In this round the 5 teams will simultaneously build a working structure similar to the prototype showcased to them. Teams will use all the waste items or junk and recycle them to make a new item. The teams will be judged according to the neatness and the working of their final item and the winner will be declared.

Robo Rugby

Robo Rugby is an event in which robots try to score a point by hitting the balls into the scoring zone at both end points of a playing table. A match of Robo Rugby

It is the time to concentrate on the hacking and slashing of the robots. So, make your bots ready and leave them at the arena to feel the game. The game of SOCCER!!

Rounds :
